Placebo – Battle For The Sun guitar tab

Posted on June 13, 2009

In my cover I play it in standard tuning and then use the a Digitech Whammy to set my guitar to a step down for one part of the song where you need to play an open D - just because it was easier for me than tuning my guitar down. The tab is in guitar pro so you should be able to transpose the tuning easily. Comment below if you think it's alright or if you have any problems, enjoy!
