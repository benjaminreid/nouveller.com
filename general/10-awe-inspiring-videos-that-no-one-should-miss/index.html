10 awe-inspiring videos that no one should miss

Posted on November 3, 2009

I've been on YouTube for a while but recently moved to another video service called Vimeo to host my screencasts, mainly because their site/player looks a lot better. What I've also found is that when you're browsing YouTube you're more likely to come across your average Joe mucking around with his camera phone rather than come across some real talent filming in glorious HD. Anyway, this is beside the point.

I've just put a set of 10 videos together that I think you or no one else should miss. Let me know what you think about them in the comments. Be sure to checkout the HD chanel on Vimeo too.

Be sure to share this collection of videos, you could use this handy short URL: http://bit.ly/10-awe-inspiring-videos
