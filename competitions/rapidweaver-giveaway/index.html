RapidWeaver giveaway

Posted on March 17, 2010

During the recent MacHeist madness I grabbed myself the first nanoBundle of the year which included a right good bunch of software including RapidWeaver. I've already got a copy of RapidWeaver so I thought why not give it back to you guys! If RapidWeaver's not what you're after then definitely stick around as I've got quite a few more software giveaways coming up.

What is RapidWeaver?

Basically, an awesome website creation tool! - "With powerful tools under the hood, yet a beautifully-familiar user interface built especially for Mac OS X Leopard, RapidWeaver is ideal for anyone looking to create a beautiful website. Whether it's your first or five-hundreth website, RapidWeaver has all the tools you need to quickly create pages you'll be proud of."

Find out more about RapidWeaver.

This is how to enter

  1. Either blog about the giveaway, tweet about, Facebook it or any other way you can think of.
  2. Once you've done that come back here and make a comment just below and leave a link to your blog post, tweet (etc...). Oh, and make sure you leave a valid email address as that's how I'll be getting in touch with the winners.

Terms of this giveaway

  • My word is final on who wins.
  • The draw will a completely random selection using a tool from random.org
  • You are allowed more than one entry depending on how many places you spread the word to. So post a link on Twitter and Facebook and you can have two entries.
  • Winner's will be chosen on the 31st of March.

We have a winner!

Congratulations to Vidit Kothari, you are the lucky winner of a copy of RapidWeaver! Here's how the draw went down. I'll be emailing you with the details tonight, and can I thank everyone who took part!
