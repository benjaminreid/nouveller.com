I’m giving away a copy of Espresso

Posted on August 21, 2009

That's right, I have gone mad, well no, I'm just feeling generous to you wonderful people who take it upon yourselves to read my blog. I'm giving away one copy of Espresso to one of you lucky people and getting your mits on it couldn't be any easier but first just to clarify I'm not giving away a cup of coffee, take a read about it first.

From the rabbit's mouth - "Espresso is not a bunch of apps thrown together. Instead, we looked at the needs of web developers and streamlined their workflow into one gorgeous, focused app. Fast and powerful editing, sleek projects, live preview, awesome publishing and extensive synchronization tools. All this, and an amazing extensible core? It's not too good to be true." You can check MacRabbit's site out for more information.

How to win

Well I said it was easy right? Here's how you can enter :

Twitter

  1. Hit the retweet button at the top of post or post on Twitter a link  to this post - http://nouveller.com/competitions/im-giving-away-a-copy-of-espresso/ or short link http://bit.ly/espressoGiveaway
  2. Then comment with your tweet status

Non-Twitter

  1. If you can post this link anywhere and show that you did then go for it and leave a comment with the link
  2. Then comment where you linked it from

OK fine, if all else fails

  • Just leave a comment and say why you'd like it!

And the winner is!

Congratulations Josh on winning yourself a copy of Espresso! The winner was chosen via random.org based on the total number of valid entries (26), here's some proof I tell ya. For those of you who didn't win, you will automatically be entered into the next giveaway and will be allowed a second entry, giving you a much better chance of winning! Stay tuned for more software giveaways!
